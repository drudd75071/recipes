# Healthier Copycat "Beefy Fritos" Burrito

This recipe is a nutrient-dense, lower-sodium, and lower-saturated-fat take on the fast-food classic. By using lean beef, a homemade spice blend, a high-fiber wrap, and store-bought corn tortilla strips, you keep the iconic savory flavor and satisfying crunch while vastly improving the nutritional profile.

## 📋 Ingredients

### For the Beef & Seasoning
* **1 lb Lean Ground Beef** (93% or 96% lean)
* **Homemade Taco Seasoning:**
  * 1 tbsp Chili powder
  * 1 tsp Ground cumin
  * ½ tsp Garlic powder
  * ½ tsp Onion powder
  * ¼ tsp Salt
* **¼ cup Water**

### For the Rice & Mix-Ins
* **2 cups Cooked Rice** (Brown rice or Cilantro-lime cauliflower rice)
* **1 Lime** (juiced)
* **¼ cup Fresh Cilantro**, finely chopped

### For Assembly
* **1 package Kraft Finely Shredded Mexican Four Cheese** (use ~2 tbsp or 1/8 cup per burrito)
* **1 bag Store-Bought Crispy Tortilla Strips** (e.g., Mission Tortilla Strips or a seasoned Santa Fe salad topper)
* **High-Fiber, Low-Carb Whole-Wheat Tortillas** (e.g., Mission Carb Balance or Olé Extreme Wellness)

---

## 🍳 Cooking Directions

### 1. Cook the Lean Beef
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the lean ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks, until completely browned (about 5–6 minutes).
3. Drain any excess liquid from the pan.
4. Stir in the **chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt**, and **¼ cup of water**. 
5. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes until the liquid reduces and coats the meat beautifully.

### 2. Season the Rice
1. While the meat is simmering, take your warm, cooked brown rice (or cauliflower rice) and place it in a bowl.
2. Toss thoroughly with the fresh **lime juice** and **chopped cilantro**.

### 3. Assemble and Sear
1. Warm a low-carb tortilla in the microwave for 10 seconds so it becomes flexible and easy to roll.
2. **Layer the Base:** Add a scoop of the cilantro-lime rice to the center of the tortilla, followed by a generous scoop of the hot seasoned beef.
3. **Add the Cheese:** Sprinkle **2 tablespoons** of the Kraft Mexican blend directly over the hot beef so it starts melting immediately.
4. **Add the Crunch:** Place a small handful of your store-bought tortilla strips right on top of the cheese layer.
5. **Roll and Sear:** Fold in the sides and roll the burrito tightly. Place it seam-side down in a hot, dry skillet over medium heat for **1–2 minutes per side**. This locks the seam shut, gives the exterior a perfectly crispy golden texture, and warms the crunchy strips inside.

---

## ⚡ Key Health Upgrades
* **Saturated Fat Reduction:** Switching from standard 80/20 ground beef to a lean 93%+ cut cuts out a massive portion of unnecessary fats while keeping the hearty beef profile.
* **Sodium Control:** Eliminating packaged taco seasoning and processed jarred nacho cheese saves hundreds of milligrams of sodium per serving.
* **Complex Carbs & Fiber:** Trading refined white flour tortillas and white rice for high-fiber alternatives slows down digestion and keeps you full much longer.
