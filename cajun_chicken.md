# Cajun Chicken Pasta

## Ingredients

### Cajun Seasoning

* 2 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp thyme
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
* 1/4 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 tsp salt

### Pasta

* 1 Tbsp olive oil
* 1 Tbsp (15 g) butter
* 1 lb (450 g) boneless, skinless chicken breast
* 1 yellow onion, diced
* 1/2 lb (230 g) penne pasta (uncooked)
* 15 oz (400 g) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
* 2 cups (500 ml) chicken broth
* 2 oz (60 g) cream cheese
* 3 green onions, sliced

---

## Instructions

### 1. Make the Cajun seasoning

Combine all Cajun seasoning ingredients in a small bowl.

### 2. Season the chicken

Cut the chicken into **1/2–3/4 inch cubes**. Place in a bowl, add the Cajun seasoning, and toss until evenly coated.

### 3. Brown the chicken

Heat olive oil and butter in a large deep skillet over **medium-high heat** until hot and foamy.

Add the chicken and cook for a couple of minutes per side until lightly browned. The chicken does **not** need to be fully cooked yet.

### 4. Cook the onion

Add the diced onion and sauté for about **2 minutes**, until it begins to soften.

Allow the onion’s moisture to loosen any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.

### 5. Add pasta and liquid

Add:

* Penne pasta
* Fire-roasted tomatoes (with juices)
* Chicken broth

Stir to combine, cover with a lid, and bring to a boil.

### 6. Simmer

Once boiling, reduce heat to **medium-low**.

Simmer for **10 minutes**, stirring every couple of minutes, until:

* Pasta is tender
* Sauce becomes thick and creamy

### 7. Finish the sauce

Add cream cheese in chunks and stir until fully melted into the sauce.

### 8. Serve

Top with sliced green onions and serve warm.
